Tides of Fate: The Guide

Objective

Your goal is to build the greatest Legacy Score by acquiring wealth, developing properties, fulfilling secret Niyyah, and earning Gratitude Tokens by helping others. The game ends the moment the Barakah Bowl hits 20 Golden Seeds.

Key Mechanics

  • The Barakah Bowl fills when players roll 3 doubles, waive rent, or trigger specific Tides of Fate events.
  • The Common Well collects charity and can reward the poorest player when someone lands on The Oasis.
  • Reflection Garden works like jail: wait 3 turns or roll doubles to leave early.
  • Waiving rent grants the owner a Gratitude Token and adds a Golden Seed.
  • Every player begins with 3 secret Niyyah cards for bonus endgame Legacy.
