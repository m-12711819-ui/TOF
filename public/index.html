Tides of Fate: The Guide

Objective

Your goal is to build the greatest **Legacy Score** by acquiring wealth, developing properties, fulfilling secret Niyyah (Intentions), and earning Gratitude Tokens by helping others. The game ends the moment the **Barakah Bowl hits 20 Golden Seeds**!

Key Mechanics

  • The Barakah Bowl: Seeds are added when players roll 3 doubles, waive rent for another player, or trigger specific Tides of Fate events. At 20 Seeds, the Era of Prosperity arrives and the game instantly ends!
  • The Common Well (Charity): Landing on Zakat/Sadaqah forces you to pay into the Well. If you land on The Oasis and the Well has 100+ Dirhams, the poorest player receives 100 Dirhams!
  • Waiving Rent: If you owe rent on an opponent's property, they can choose to **Waive** the fee. This grants them a Gratitude Token (worth massive points at the end) and adds a Golden Seed to the bowl!
  • Reflection Garden: If you roll 3 doubles or land on 'Go To Reflection', you are sent here. You must wait 3 turns OR roll doubles to leave early.
  • Secret Niyyah Cards: Every player starts with 3 hidden objective cards. Fulfill their confusing or peculiar tasks quietly throughout the game for massive bonus points when the game ends. Keep them secret!
  • Tides of Fate: Landing on these spaces triggers server-wide global events. Some help everyone, some trigger recessions, and some dump seeds into the Barakah Bowl.
